Keep the entire village connected.

It takes a village to raise a child — parents, relatives, teachers, coaches — but it can be hard for everyone to stay connected. Portfoliyo is a place to bring a community together around each child's learning experiences, to provide the support they need.

  • Build a private Portfoliyo village. Get parents, teachers, mentors, instructors, and administrators all on board.
  • Teachers: Share notes, photos, and documents with each student's village. Show what they are learning, and ask for help when you (or they) need it!
  • Parents: Stay informed and in touch. Participate in the lives and educations of the children you care about. Give them your encouragement and feedback to help them learn and grow!
