{# Features ------------------------------------- #}

Two-way communication

Frequent and streamlined contact keeps parents & teachers united around the education of every child. Compliment a student's work, send updates about assignments & conferences, intervene quickly to change behavior, or just stay available for parent questions.

Invite other teachers to join the conversation.

Keep the entire faculty in the loop about every student. Add multiple family members to the conversation. Mom, Dad, Grandma, Grandpa...

Easily sign up parents.

Send home a sign-up sheet as homework with your students, and parents take care of the rest.
